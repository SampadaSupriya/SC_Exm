nmap commands

simple scan
nmap -sn <target ip>

port scan
nmap -F <target ip>

first 1000 tcp port scan
nmap <target ip>

SYN scan(half open scan)
nmap -sS <target ip>

TCP scan
nmap -sT <target ip>

UDP
nmap -sU <target ip>

all ports 65535
nmap -p- <target ip>

version
nmap -sV <target ip>

OS
nmap -O <target ip>

all OS,version 
nmap -A <target ip>

safe script scan
nmap -sC <target ip>

vulnerability scan for target
nmap --script vuln <target ip>

malware scan
nmap --script malware <target ip>


firewall bypass
nmap -f <target ip>

Decoy Scan -mix fake ips with real ip
nmap -D <fake ip1>,<fake ip2>,<attacker ip> <target ip>

verbose sequential scan
nmap -v -r <target ip>

skip host discovery
nmap -Pn <target ip>


ICMP echo ping
nmap -PE <target ip>
